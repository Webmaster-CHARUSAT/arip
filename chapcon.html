CHAPCON 2025 – National Physiotherapy Conference

CHAPCON 2025 aims to advance physiotherapy through research, innovation, and collaboration. The two-day event will feature keynote addresses, panel discussions, paper/poster presentations, and demonstration sessions focusing on rehabilitation, integration of new technologies, and translating global evidence into context-specific practice.

Objectives

 

Disseminate the latest research in rehabilitation sciences.

 

Provide opportunities for networking and collaboration.

 

 

Subtheme

 

Transforming ideas into real-world solutions

 

Translating global evidence into context-specific practice

 

Illuminating pathways in rehabilitation and recovery
